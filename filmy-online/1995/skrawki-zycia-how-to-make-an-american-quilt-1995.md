Skrawki życia / How to Make an American Quilt 1995 
=============
[![Skrawki życia / How to Make an American Quilt 1995 ](http://vidos.pl/images/player.gif)](http://vidos.pl/skrawki-zycia-how-to-make-an-american-quilt-1995)
